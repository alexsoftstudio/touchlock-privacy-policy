Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Touch Lock: Video Screen Blocker ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The app DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No name, email, or contact information is requested.
  • No device identifiers are sent to our servers.
  • All app settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Accessibility Service Usage

The app requires the Accessibility Service API to function. This is vital for the following features:

  • Touch Blocking: To intercept and ignore touch events while the lock is active.
  • System Button Interception: To prevent accidental exits via the Home, Back, or Recents buttons during video playback.

Important: We do not use the Accessibility Service to monitor your activity, read screen content, or collect any private data. It is used strictly for its technical ability to manage touch events.

3. Notification Policy (Notification Shield)

If enabled, the app requests access to "Do Not Disturb" settings. This is used solely to hide incoming pop-up notifications while the screen is locked, preventing accidental interaction with other apps.

4. OLED Eco Mode

This mode displays a black overlay to protect OLED screens from burn-in and save battery. The wandering clock feature is used purely for screen health protection (pixel shifting).

5. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability. This service may collect technical data such as crash logs and device models to help us fix bugs. This data is anonymous and not linked to any individual user.
